Origin Story

Just like any college student, I had been working at a restaurant while I was studying for my undergraduate degree in Architecture at UC San diego in California. But I differed from my classmates in that I never saw myself actually becoming an architect or working in an office environment. My goal was really just to graduate. I also never anticipated opening a restaurant. So in a sense, the restaurant came to me.
